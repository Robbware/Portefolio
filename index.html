Pace Granny

Pace Granny's an infinite runner made for tablets and cellphones where the player plays as an old grandma trying to run away from death. During her run, she's able to obtain powerups and debuffs which may or may not help her achieve her goal. The player must also keep her pacemaker beating to the rhythm of the song.
Made in 36h.

  • Engine: Unity 5
  • Date: January 2017
  • Service: Game Development

Living Again

Living Again's a game about a FBI Agent investigating a very peculiar case. To do this, the Agent, controlled by the player, must find and understand the different clues scattered around the forest and the cabin until he realises the truth behind the murder that had happened.

  • Engine: Unity 5
  • Date: June 2016
  • Service: Game Development

A* Implementation

Unnamed Prototype

This prototype was made as a proof-of-concept for a freelancing work. This prototype allows the player to test his reaction time while storing all the data regarding the test. This was not meant as a game but rather as a tool in order to evaluate different users.

  • Client: Unnamed
  • Engine: Unity 5
  • Date: April 2017
  • Service: Software/Game Development

Fear Reality

An Android/iOS VR game where you have to face your deepest fears in order to progress. In order to complete the game you have to face Aracnophobia, Arcophobia, Bathophobia, Claustrophobia and Lynophobia. Made in 36h.

  • Engine: Unity 5
  • Date: April 2017
  • Service: Game Development
  • Download: Android APK (Requires Google Cardboard)

TRIBE

A Doodle-god like game where the player has to obtain and combine several different elements to obtain the right elements in order for the player character to return home. The catch is that the player has only 9 in-game days that get shorter day after day, starting at 180 seconds, and every time a new day is up you forget all of your combinations so far and the final goal changes.
Made in under 24h.

  • Engine: Unity 5
  • Date: May 2015
  • Service: Game Development
  • Download: N/A
